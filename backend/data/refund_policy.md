# Synthetic E-commerce Refund Policy

## General Rules
All refunds are processed back to the original method of payment. Shipping costs are non-refundable.

## Category-Specific Rules

### 1. Electronics
- **Return Window**: 14 days from the purchase date.
- **Condition**: Must be in original packaging with all accessories included.

### 2. Clothing
- **Return Window**: 30 days from the purchase date.
- **Condition**: Must be unworn, unwashed, and have original tags attached.

### 3. Software & Digital Downloads
- **Return Window**: Strictly **non-refundable**.
- **Condition**: Once a software license or digital download link is issued, it cannot be revoked, making it ineligible for return.

### 4. Final Sale / Clearance
- **Return Window**: Strictly **non-refundable**.
- **Condition**: Items purchased on clearance or marked "Final Sale" are not eligible for refunds or exchanges.
